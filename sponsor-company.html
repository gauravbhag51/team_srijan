“Team Srijan is more than just a team - it is a legacy. A legacy of students from various disciples, bound together by one common goal to test their knowledge and build a race car. We have been participating in national and international formula student competitions since 2007, continuing to innovate each year to make our designs better and our cars faster.

This year has been the most difficult, but we remain undeterred. Despite the pandemic and the many challenges it posed, we ranked 4th in the virtual Qualifying Rule Quiz for Formula Bharat 2021. Now, we are entirely focused on designing our first electric vehicle and are strategizing to deliver the best results in the virtual Formula Bharat 2021.

The team has been supported by industry leaders like Gedore, SAIL, IOCL, Jiwago Precision Dies, Magod Lasers, TATA Steel, Punjab National Bank, NTPC, Aditya Birla Group, Jindal Steel & Power Limited, Usha Martin, and Atul limited. among many others. Their investment in the team (monetary, in-kind, educational, or logistical) has led us closer to achieving our goals. We can never thank them enough for helping us pave the path to all our success.

On behalf of the team, I want to thank all our sponsors and supporters for encouraging our dream and fuelling our passion. We are grateful for your outstanding support that has kept us motivated to continue to pursue our dream.

If you are interested in our project and want to support us, please navigate to Our Brochure to know more.”

-Aditya Singh Chauhan
(Team Captain)
